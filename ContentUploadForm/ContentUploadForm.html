CL Subscription Pilot Program


Curious Learning is looking at a model where it would charge deployments that do not meet the criteria of need listed above a subscription fee for access to bundles of commercial content. By checking this box you are agreeing to the following compensation if your content is included in one of these bundles:

You would get a percentage of a monthly, annual, or quarterly subscription fee. For example: if Curious Learning received $5 per tablet (after costs), and your app was used 20% of the time amongst all the apps in the bundle, then you get 0.2*$5 = $1 per tablet.

This agreement pertains only to the app you are currently uploading. If you wish to enroll more apps in the program, you must individually enroll them at the time of upload, or through your Content Partner Account if they have already been uploaded.

You are free to remove your content from this pilot program at any time. If you remove it, it will be removed for existing subscriptions at the beginning of the next renewed cycle.
